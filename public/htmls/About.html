About Us

EDUBASE was founded in the year 2007 by the Late Chief Obieze Mattew. This was a time in the country where standard education was been sought after and he did the set the pace most schools in the region use.
We are more than pace setters, we are the leaders in the field from Arts to Sciences and Techology with state of the art laboratories and research facilities.
We are the lead base of ICT in Lagos Nigeria.

What we offer

Effective Student Data Management
We help your School manage their database by making the admin interface easy to add and edit student information
State of the Art labs for research
With the poor standard of Schools this days we have laboratories to conduct school practical
Also give project students and scientists a standard lab for research
Conducive Study Enviroment
Ranging from libraries to couryards and study centers for tutorials, the enviroment is conducive for the serious minded
